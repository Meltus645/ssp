How SSPA Works

Client

Search for service

Enter the service you want into the searchbox. You can also select from the suggestions and hit search

Chose a provider

You can contact the provider from a list of providers listed to make arrangements to enjoy the service.

Review service

Leave a review about the provider and help us understand how you were served.

Provider

Register Service

Provide the information about your service including the brand name, contacts details, price and location.

Engage the client

Clients contact providers using the contact information provided when registering the service.

Sell Service

Reach an agreement and make point to attend to the client professionaly

Join the leaders board

Rank high on SSP leaders board from the positive client reviews to frequent the searches
